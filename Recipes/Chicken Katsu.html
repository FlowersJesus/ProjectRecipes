Chicken Katsu

Chicken Katsu is a Japanese-style fried chicken.

Ingredients

    Chicken: You'll need four skinless boneless chicken breast halves
    Seasonings: This chicken recipe will use salt and pepper
    Egg: An egg adds moisture and gives the Panko crumbs something to stick to.
    Panko: Panko bread crumbs are responsible for the signature crunch.
    Oil: Opt for a neutral oil with a high smoke point, I recommend canola or vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Season the chicken, then dredge in flour.
  2. Coat each breast in egg, then press into the Panko.
  3. Fry the chicken katsu until golden brown.
  4. Enjoy with Tonkatsu sauce
